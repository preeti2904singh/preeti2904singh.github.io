Global Settings

A comprehensive solution for all your account settings.

Problem

The global settings page for the TLM app is intricate and continuously evolving with new features. However, feedback from both customers and internal teams indicates that the navigation structure is not effectively communicated. By enhancing the clarity of the Information Architecture (IA), we can significantly improve user experience and satisfaction.

Goal
  • Enhance the navigation to align with how our customers think and navigate.
  • Reorganize the global settings page to present options clearly, ensuring that the most commonly used features are readily accessible.
  • Empower users by providing direct pathways to specific settings from other areas of the application, creating a seamless experience.

Solution
  • We believe that removing the settings options from the high-level navigation will simplify the information architecture in a way that meets customer expectations.
  • By grouping all settings options on a single page, users will be able to quickly identify where to navigate to resolve their issues.
  • Additionally, providing users access to various settings pages throughout the application is likely to increase adoption and retention by simplifying the setup process.


Project duration: 2 weeks

Test phase

Usability testing

After completing the visual design, I conducted user testing on the prototype to evaluate the user-friendliness of the global settings workflow. During a series of Zoom calls, I observed participants as they tackled specific tasks within the application, gaining valuable insights into their experiences and interactions with the interface.


Tasks performed
  • How can a profile be created?
  • What exactly do you understand by renewal flows?
  • How are the Subject domain name and Subject Alternate Name configured?
  • Where would you go if you wanted to change your admin contact information?
  • How do you add fields for authentication enrollment?


Findings from the user testing

  • Users were easily able to understand the contextual button provided on the profile page as to how they can create a profile from a template.
  • With the help of info text users where able to understand what renewal flows was all about.
  • Users were happy about the tooltips we provided to give a summary of what each step contains.
  • Users were able to configure the Subject domain name and Subject Alternate Name.

Final conclusion

According to our analysis, the profile workflow scored 87 on the Usability Scale, which is great! Based on customer feedback and internal plan goals, we have been continuously upgrading since the launch in 2020.
